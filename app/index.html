Mesh Demand & Bounty System

Mesh Service Bounties

Service bounties are publicly created bounties in which anyone can request service. By requesting service in a bounty, you can specify how much you want to pay to have a provider initiate service at a location. Providers can claim bounties by registering, then physically start service within a bounty location

Mesh Service Requsts

Service Requests

Anyone that wants meshnet service can submit a request for service. This requires the requester to fund a smart contract with tokens. Tokens will be put in escrow until a service provider begins service, or a time limit has expired. Escrow will be used as prepaid service and will pay the service provider initially Requester can add a service bonuse to encourage initial setup and start of service. The higher the amount in bonus + escrow, the more likely a service provider will service the request.
