Activity

This tool reports transaction activity between service provider nodes and customers on the blockchain. Active transactions are the primary indicator that service providers are doing their job in a given location. Downtrending or inactivity entirely indicate that customers are leaving quickly. May represent opportunity for a provider to come in and help provide better service. Our Mesh Service Opportunities tool helps distill the data down to important opportunities.
